Goodshuffle Pro · Social & paid copy framework · 2026

Taglines & copy by vertical, persona, and platform

Full-funnel copy direction for all six verticals, mapped to buyer personas, creative formats, and platforms. Built from the Vertical Strategy + Segmentation Systems doc, Selling to Each Persona, and the 2026 Content Strategy. Applies the Audience-Naming Rule, Tier 2 dog-whistle vocabulary, and the desire axis.

TOFU — awareness, scroll-stop, punchy
MOFU — consideration, trust, specificity
BOFU — demo/trial conversion, grounded + direct
Production rule — Per the Audience-Naming Rule (AG April 2026): at least 4 of 5 headlines and 4 of 5 primary text variations must name the audience explicitly. Generic nouns get vertical prefixes ("business" → "event rental business," "gear" → "AV gear"). Every copy line in this doc passes the isolation test: it could only be for the intended vertical.

Plan tier overlay — Lite vs. Standard
Messaging principle — Solo operators (Lite, ~$39/mo) don't want to be told they're "small." Lead with permission and professionalism, not aspiration or "get bigger" framing. Standard messaging leads with team coordination, growth, and scaling without breaking.
